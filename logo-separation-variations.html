My recommendation

V1 (Quiet gap) or V3 (Tagline strip).

V1 is the cleanest expression of "wordmark on top, nav below" — confident and editorial. V3 is the same shape but earns its breathing room with a small veteran-owned · founder-led · global meta strip, which doubles as a brand signal on first impression. V6 is the boldest option if you want the top of the page to feel branded right away. V2 and V4 are good middle grounds; V5 adds a hint of color separation without a full banner.
