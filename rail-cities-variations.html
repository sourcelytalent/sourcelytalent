Rail cities — lowercase

Five takes on lowercasing the city list in the floating nav. Each preserves the same Sourcely chrome (icon, pill, CTA) so only the city treatment changes. Pick one and I'll wire it into the real nav.

v1 Teal nodes (current) Same pattern as today, just lowercase. Most familiar.
nyc orlando barcelona toronto bogotá zurich
v2 Mid-dot separators Clean, classic — reads like a dateline.
nyc· orlando· barcelona· toronto· bogotá· zurich
v3 Wide-tracked minimalist Editorial, quiet, very lowercase. Lets the wordmark breathe.
nyc orlando barcelona toronto bogotá zurich
v4 Italic slash separators Slightly editorial — slashes echo the serif italic in the brand.
nyc/ orlando/ barcelona/ toronto/ bogotá/ zurich
v5 Network flow (→) Implies a connected global network — most expressive option.
nyc→ orlando→ barcelona→ toronto→ bogotá→ zurich
v6 Brand lens as separator Echoes the magnifying-glass mark you use as the “e” in display type. Most on-brand.
nyc orlando barcelona toronto bogotá zurich
v7 Italic ampersand Quiet editorial nod — Fraunces italic & in teal between cities.
nyc& orlando& barcelona& toronto& bogotá& zurich
v8 Italic serif cities Cities rendered in Fraunces italic — mirrors the emphasis words in your headlines.
nyc· orlando· barcelona· toronto· bogotá· zurich
v9 Teal underline tag Each city tagged with a teal hairline underline — active, tactile, like a link.
nyc orlando barcelona toronto bogotá zurich
