Nav logo — three options

Comparing how the logo can sit in the floating nav. Forefront uses a horizontal wordmark (option 2 below replicates that pattern in HTML/type for crispness at any size).

Option 1 · Wordmark image

Uses your existing horizontal logo file (logo.jpg). Has a white background, so the nav becomes solid white instead of the teal ombré.

Option 2 · Icon + typeset wordmark (Forefront style)

Keeps the round icon, adds "Sourcely Talent" set in Quicksand alongside it. Crisp at any size, matches your logo's font, and works on the gradient background.
